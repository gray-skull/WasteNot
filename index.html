    About WasteNot

    WasteNot is a web application that helps users reduce food waste by providing recipes based on the ingredients they have at home. By using WasteNot, you can save money, reduce your carbon footprint, and help the environment.

    Save money

    Use up the ingredients you already have at home instead of buying new ones

    Reduce waste

    Prevent food from going bad and ending up in the trash

    Help the environment

    Reduce your carbon footprint and contribute to a more sustainable planet
